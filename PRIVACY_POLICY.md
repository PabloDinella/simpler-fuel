# Privacy Policy for Simpler Fuel

**Last Updated: January 25, 2026**

## Introduction

Simpler Fuel ("we", "our", or "us") is committed to protecting your privacy. This Privacy Policy explains how we collect, use, disclose, and safeguard your information when you use our mobile application and services.

## Information We Collect

### Personal Information

When you create an account, we collect:
- **Email address**: Used for account creation, authentication, and account recovery
- **Password**: Stored securely using industry-standard encryption (we never store passwords in plain text)

### Fuel Tracking Data

When you use the app to track your fuel consumption, we collect:
- **Fuel entry records**: Including date, odometer readings, fuel volume, and optional notes
- **User preferences**: Language settings, unit preferences (distance and volume units), and consumption format preferences

### Technical Information

We may automatically collect:
- **Device information**: Device type, operating system version
- **Usage data**: App performance metrics, crash reports (if any)
- **Timestamps**: When entries are created or modified

## How We Use Your Information

We use the collected information for the following purposes:

1. **Service Provision**
   - Create and manage your account
   - Store and synchronize your fuel tracking data across devices
   - Calculate fuel consumption statistics and trends
   - Provide offline functionality with cloud synchronization

2. **Service Improvement**
   - Analyze usage patterns to improve app performance
   - Fix bugs and technical issues
   - Develop new features based on user needs

3. **Communication**
   - Send account-related notifications (e.g., password reset emails)
   - Respond to your inquiries and support requests

## Data Storage and Security

### Where Your Data is Stored

- **Local Storage**: All your data is first stored locally on your device using encrypted IndexedDB
- **Cloud Storage**: When you are online and authenticated, your data is synchronized with our secure cloud database powered by Supabase (hosted on secure servers)
- **Data Center Location**: Cloud data is stored in Supabase data centers (location depends on your Supabase instance configuration)

### How We Protect Your Data

We implement appropriate technical and organizational security measures:
- **Encryption in transit**: All data transmitted between your device and our servers uses HTTPS/TLS encryption
- **Secure authentication**: Passwords are hashed and salted using industry-standard algorithms
- **Row-level security**: Your fuel entries are protected by database-level security policies ensuring you can only access your own data
- **Regular security updates**: We keep our dependencies and infrastructure up to date with the latest security patches

## Data Sharing and Disclosure

We do **NOT** sell, trade, or rent your personal information to third parties.

We may share your information only in the following limited circumstances:

1. **Service Providers**: We use Supabase as our backend infrastructure provider for authentication, database, and real-time synchronization services
2. **Legal Requirements**: If required by law, court order, or governmental authority
3. **Protection of Rights**: To protect our rights, privacy, safety, or property, and that of our users

## Third-Party Services

Our app uses the following third-party service:

- **Supabase**: Provides authentication, database, and real-time synchronization services
  - Privacy Policy: https://supabase.com/privacy
  - Terms of Service: https://supabase.com/terms

We do not integrate any advertising networks, analytics services, or social media platforms.

## Your Data Rights

You have the following rights regarding your personal data:

### Access and Portability
- You can access all your fuel entry data within the app
- You can export your data at any time through the app's interface

### Modification
- You can edit or update your fuel entries at any time
- You can modify your preferences and settings

### Deletion
- You can delete individual fuel entries
- You can request account deletion at: **https://simplerfuel.app/delete-account**
- You can also delete your entire account and all associated data by contacting us at support@simplerfuel.app
- Upon account deletion, all your data will be permanently removed from our servers within 30 days

For detailed information about account deletion, please visit our [Account Deletion Page](https://simplerfuel.app/delete-account) which provides:
- Step-by-step instructions for deleting your account
- Complete list of data types that will be deleted
- Information about data retention periods
- Alternative methods to request deletion

### Data Control
- **Offline Mode**: You can use the app entirely offline without cloud synchronization
- **Sync Control**: You choose when to enable cloud synchronization by signing in

## Data Retention

- **Active Accounts**: We retain your data for as long as your account is active
- **Deleted Accounts**: Data is permanently deleted within 30 days of account deletion
- **Inactive Accounts**: Accounts inactive for more than 2 years may be subject to deletion after notification

## Children's Privacy

Simpler Fuel is not intended for use by children under the age of 13. We do not knowingly collect personal information from children under 13. If you are a parent or guardian and believe your child has provided us with personal information, please contact us, and we will delete such information.

## Offline Functionality

Our app is designed with offline-first functionality:
- All data is stored locally on your device first
- Cloud synchronization only occurs when you are authenticated and online
- You can use all core features without an internet connection
- Local data remains on your device even when offline
